Solution: Meta 2: In Town
Answer: DETACHED BUILDING

Written by aki and Jonathan

There are 3 separate meta puzzles taken from the normal puzzle round. After solving the prepuzzle, we have found that Waldo went back to three scenes in his very first book. Subsequently, we visit 26 locations, each of which belongs to one of the three given scenes.

As clued by the flavor text, we should split the two words from each answer, and we notice that the two words in each answer are the same length. Furthermore, the second word from each answer consists of all unique letters, except for one pair of repeated letters.

Since the two words in each answer are of the same length, we extract the letter pair in the corresponding positions from the first word, which gives us the second location where Waldo can be found, the DETACHED BUILDING.
