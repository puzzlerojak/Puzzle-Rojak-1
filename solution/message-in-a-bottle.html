Solution: Message in a Bottle
Answer: LETTER FROM HAWAII

Written by aki

To start, we might note that the flavor text tells us that we found these images are cutouts of a larger image. With some searching, we find that these are parts of stamps from around the world. (This can also be found relatively easily by searching the first set of images up.) After identifying a couple of the stamps, we find that every (picture) image in the first set matches with a (caption) image in the second set. In addition, the caption lines of each stamp are missing one letter from the corresponding original stamp.

We note that the first set of images are ordered alphabetically by their country of origin (which is also the order given in the table). The second set of images are ordered alphabetically by the line in their caption (ignoring numbers).
