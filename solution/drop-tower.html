At this point, we might notice that the letters that fell into the wrong columns from each grid all lie in different columns. This prompts us to read them from left to right, and in grid order: IUSCSRUUIOTLRDRASEEF. Even though this does not spell anything, we notice that the blanks at the bottom have space for 20 letters (since green cells take two letters). This results in the following grid.

IUSCSRUUIOTLRDRASEEF

What is left to do is simply transform the grid based on the rules for each type of colored cell. Following the instructions for each type of cell results in the final answer, SUFFERING PROCESS.
