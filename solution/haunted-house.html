After identifying the theme and the extracted words, we can notice that the flavor text says there are many “scary things” in the house. This means that all of the answers, and by extension their theme, might be found to be scary. This clues us to find out who is scared of each of these things. In particular, we want to find the name for each of these fears, a word with suffix -phobia.
